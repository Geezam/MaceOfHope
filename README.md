**Mace of Hope** is a text-based, first-person dungeon crawler RPG written in Python.

Heavily inspired by the classic Game Boy title *The Sword of Hope* (1989), this project combines retro exploration mechanics with a modern, colorful console interface powered by the [Rich](https://github.com/Textualize/rich) library.

It is built upon the RPG battle engine developed in [SimpleRPG2](https://github.com/Geezam/SimpleRPG2).

* **First-Person Exploration:** Navigate a dark fantasy world using cardinal directions (North, South, East, West).
* **Visual Interface:** A split-screen UI that renders text descriptions alongside retro-style ASCII art for every location.
* **Turn-Based Combat:** Engage in classic RPG battles. Your speed determines turn order, and stats (Vitality, Dexterity, Intelligence) determine the outcome.
* **Loot & Equipment:** Defeat monsters to earn gold and find loot. Equip weapons, armor, and accessories to boost your stats.
* **Dynamic Stats:** Character stats (HP, Attack, Defense, Evasion) are calculated in real-time based on your class, level, and current gear.
